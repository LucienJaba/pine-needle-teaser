What We Do

Our Services

From custom embroidery to advanced digital printing, we bring your brand to life on premium apparel.

Custom Embroidery

Precision stitching on any garment. Priced by stitch count with setup fees averaging $60. Discounts available on orders over 75 pieces.

Direct to Garment

High-resolution prints directly on cotton blends. Perfect for detailed designs, photos, and full-color graphics.

Sublimation Print

Vibrant, all-over prints on polyester and rayon fabrics. Colors that last and won't crack, peel, or fade.

Heat Transfer Print

Versatile printing on various fabrics. Great for budget-friendly projects with vibrant, durable results.

Screen Printing

Classic screen printing for bulk orders. Bold colors and sharp lines ideal for team gear and merchandise.

Custom Patches

Iron-on and sew-on options. Perfect for uniforms, caps, and bags. Die-cut vinyl also available.

Our Story

Meet Lisa Foster

Owner & Founder — Lisa has been at the helm of Pine Needle Embroidery for over three decades, building a trusted name in custom apparel from the heart of Teton Valley.

At Pine Needle Embroidery, custom apparel is not just apparel — it's a representation of your business, school, cause, family, team, or special event. We've built our reputation over three decades of delivering quality, customer satisfaction, and reliability.

Operating from our 3,600 square foot, state-of-the-art production facility in Driggs, Idaho, we handle everything in-house — from digitizing your logo to the final stitch. This gives us complete quality control and the ability to deliver fast turnaround times.

We're proud to be an approved embroidery shop for The North Face and a trusted partner of STIO. Our work has reached clients across the United States and internationally, including London, Ontario, Manitoba, and Mumbai.

We offer Screen Printing, Direct to Garment Printing on cotton blends, Sublimation Printing on polyester and rayon, Heat Transfer Printing on various fabrics, Die-cut Vinyl, and Custom Patches (iron-on and sew-on).

Embroidery is priced by stitch count with an average setup fee of $60. Individual sew runs approximately $10 with a minimum charge of $8.50. Discounts are available on orders exceeding 75 pieces. Digital printing starts around $6.00 for chest logos and $14-$16 for larger back designs.

Absolutely! We have established relationships with businesses across the United States and internationally, including London, Ontario, Manitoba, and Mumbai. We offer free shipping within the contiguous U.S. and UPS partnerships for international delivery.

Our general turnaround time is 10-15 business days from receiving your product to being ready for pickup or shipping. During the Christmas season, turnaround extends to 30-45 days. We can accommodate tighter timelines whenever possible.
